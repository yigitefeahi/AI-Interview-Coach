# Defense & Aerospace Interview Question Seeds

## Aselsan / Havelsan — systems and software

- Describe integrating a subsystem where failure has high operational impact. How did you verify correctness?
- Explain your test strategy for mission-critical software vs non-critical modules.
- How do you handle requirements changes while keeping verification evidence intact?
- Walk through defect found late in cycle—root cause and prevention added.
- How do you document design decisions for audit or certification review?

## TUSAŞ / aerospace engineering

- Present a design tradeoff that required cross-disciplinary review (hardware, software, systems).
- How do you manage configuration management across firmware, software, and test benches?
- Describe a safety or quality concern you raised and resolution path.
- Explain verification vs validation in a program you supported.

## Security and compliance

- How is access controlled on classified or sensitive development environments?
- Describe handling of security findings in release gate process.
- How do you balance delivery pressure with mandatory quality gates?

## Behavioral

- Tell me about working under strict process without blocking reasonable progress.
- Share a time you disagreed on risk acceptance for a release.
- Describe coordinating with external partner or supplier on integration delays.

## Technical probes

- How do you test timing-critical or embedded components?
- Explain redundancy or failover in a system you worked on.
- What traceability do you maintain from requirement to test case?
- How do you handle long-running soak tests and flaky hardware environments?

## Adaptive follow-ups

- What evidence would an auditor ask for and did you have it ready?
- What was your personal deliverable vs team deliverable?
- What metric improved (defect escape, test coverage, mean time to detect)?
