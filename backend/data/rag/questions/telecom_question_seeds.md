# Telecom Interview Question Seeds

## Turkcell — digital product and scale

- Design customer-facing service staying available during NYE or campaign peak.
- Tell me about improving latency, conversion, churn, or support ticket volume with evidence.
- How do you run experiments on subscriber-facing features without harming trust?
- Describe mobile app release process and rollback for critical auth path.

## Türk Telekom — enterprise and infrastructure

- Explain large-scale migration or modernization with reduced rollout risk.
- How do you integrate legacy BSS/OSS with modern digital channels?
- Describe stakeholder alignment across network, IT, and product orgs.
- Tell me about SLA definition and reporting for enterprise customers.

## Network and platform reliability

- How would you design provisioning API for SIM activation at high throughput?
- Explain handling downstream dependency failure in customer self-service portal.
- What observability do you need for prepaid top-up and billing adjacency systems?
- Design rate limiting and abuse protection for public telecom APIs.

## Behavioral

- Migration cutover weekend—your role and outcome.
- Conflict between marketing launch date and engineering readiness.
- Incident affecting subscribers during peak—communication and fix.

## Technical probes

- BFF vs direct microservice calls for customer dashboard—tradeoffs.
- Cache strategy for subscriber profile reads; consistency expectations.
- How do you load-test auth and top-up flows before national events?
- Circuit breaker patterns when personalization service degrades.

## Adaptive follow-ups

- Subscriber or business metric before/after?
- What degraded intentionally to protect core path?
- Rollback tested how many times before cutover?
