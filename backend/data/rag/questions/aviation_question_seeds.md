# Aviation & Travel Tech Interview Question Seeds

## Booking and search

- How would you design flight search that stays fast during holiday peaks?
- Explain seat hold TTL and preventing oversell across search, hold, and pay.
- What happens when fare cache is stale vs PSS inventory—how do you reconcile?
- Design multi-city or connecting itinerary search at high level.
- How do you handle currency, locale, and tax display for international routes?

## Check-in, mobile, and passenger experience

- Walk through mobile check-in with boarding pass and seat change edge cases.
- How do you design flows for IRROPS (delays, cancellations, rebooking)?
- Push notifications during disruption—how avoid spamming passengers?
- Payment for baggage or seat upgrade mid-flow—failure and retry UX.

## Operations and reliability

- Describe preparing systems for bayram / peak travel campaign.
- Tell me about an incident affecting travelers during peak—mitigation and long-term fix.
- How do you coordinate with ops, call center, and marketing on major releases?
- What SLOs matter for booking vs search vs check-in?

## Revenue and cost (Pegasus-style)

- Explain tradeoff between conversion optimization and operational cost.
- How would you detect and fix abandoned booking funnel drop at payment step?
- Dynamic pricing adjacency—how engineering supports revenue teams safely.

## Behavioral

- Share a time passenger-visible downtime occurred—your role and outcome.
- Describe working under time pressure before peak season go-live.
- Tell me about reducing support load through product or engineering change.
- How do you balance speed with safety in travel-critical systems?

## Adaptive follow-ups

- What passenger or business metric moved (conversion, call volume, NPS)?
- How did you load-test or game-day before peak?
- What rollback plan existed for customer-facing release?
