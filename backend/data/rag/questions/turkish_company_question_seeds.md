# Turkish Company Interview Question Seeds

Aselsan / Havelsan / defense-technology:
- Describe a system you helped integrate where failure would have high operational impact. How did you verify correctness?
- How do you balance delivery pressure with quality gates and documentation requirements?
- Tell me about a defect found late in the cycle and how you prevented recurrence.

TUSAŞ / aerospace:
- Explain a design or software decision that required cross-disciplinary review. What tradeoffs did you present?
- How do you handle changing requirements while maintaining verification evidence?
- Describe a quality or safety concern you raised and how the team resolved it.

Turkcell / Türk Telekom / telecom:
- How would you design a customer-facing service that must stay available during peak events?
- Tell me about improving a metric such as latency, conversion, churn, or ticket volume with evidence.
- Describe a migration or modernization project and how you reduced rollout risk.

Trendyol / Hepsiburada / e-commerce:
- How would you handle duplicate orders or inventory inconsistencies in a high-traffic checkout flow?
- Tell me about preparing a system for a major campaign or peak sale. What broke and what did you fix permanently?
- Describe an experiment that improved conversion or seller/buyer experience with measurable results.

Getir / quick-commerce:
- Explain how you improved dispatch, routing, or operational efficiency under tight delivery windows.
- Tell me about an incident during peak demand and your mitigation plus long-term fix.

Garanti BBVA / Akbank / banking:
- How would you design a payment or transfer flow to prevent duplicate charges and support reconciliation?
- Tell me about a production issue affecting customer transactions and how you restored trust.
- Describe how you balanced feature speed with security, audit, or compliance requirements.

Papara / fintech:
- Explain idempotency and fraud controls in a wallet top-up or P2P transfer feature you worked on.
- How do you monitor and roll back a bad release on a money-moving critical path?
- Tell me about improving activation, payment success rate, or support ticket volume with evidence.

Peak Games / Dream Games / mobile gaming:
- Describe a performance or stability improvement on mobile with before/after metrics.
- How did you ship or operate a live event or content update without increasing crash rate?
- Tell me about an A/B test or player-metric change that informed a design or engineering decision.

Pegasus / aviation:
- How would you prepare a booking or check-in service for a holiday peak without downtime?
- Describe an incident that affected travelers and your short-term fix plus permanent prevention.
- Explain a change that improved conversion, mobile reliability, or operational cost with numbers.

General Turkish company follow-ups:
- What was your personal contribution versus the team's?
- What metric proved the change worked?
- How did you communicate with non-technical stakeholders in Turkish or English?
