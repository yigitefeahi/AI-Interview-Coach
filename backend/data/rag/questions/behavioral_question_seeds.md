# Behavioral Interview Question Seeds

Conflict and collaboration:
- Tell me about a time you disagreed with a teammate or manager. How did you resolve it?
- Describe a situation where you had to influence others without formal authority.
- Give an example of receiving tough feedback and what you changed afterward.

Ownership and impact:
- Tell me about a project you owned end-to-end. What went wrong and how did you recover?
- Describe a decision you made with incomplete information. How did you validate it?
- Share a time you improved a metric or customer outcome. How did you measure success?

Failure and learning:
- Tell me about a mistake you made in production or delivery. What did you learn?
- Describe a project that did not meet expectations. What would you do differently now?

Leadership and communication:
- How do you prioritize when everything seems urgent?
- Tell me about explaining a technical decision to a non-technical stakeholder.

Adaptive follow-ups when user memory shows weak metrics:
- What number changed because of your action?
- What would have happened if you had done nothing?
- Who specifically benefited from the outcome?

Adaptive follow-ups when structure is weak:
- What was the situation in one sentence?
- What was your exact task versus the team's task?
- What actions did you personally take, in order?
