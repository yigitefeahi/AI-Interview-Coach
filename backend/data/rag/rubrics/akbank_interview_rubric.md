# Akbank Interview Rubric Signals

Akbank and technology subsidiary loops (including digital engineering teams) emphasize secure, reliable banking systems, strong data integrity, and delivery in a large regulated institution. Candidates should show respect for controls while still driving measurable product or platform outcomes.

Technical interviews may probe API design for banking services, mobile/web channels, data pipelines, anti-fraud adjacency, cloud migration, or integration with legacy core systems. Strong answers include non-functional requirements first: correctness, availability, traceability, and security. Weak answers focus on frameworks without explaining financial risk or operational recovery.

Behavioral answers should show collaboration with business, risk, and operations teams, persistence on long programs, and accountability during incidents affecting customers or transactions.

Red flags: no rollback strategy, vague impact, dismissing compliance constraints, or team-only stories without personal ownership.

Coaching tips: quantify reliability improvements, fraud catch rate, release quality, or customer digital adoption. Explain how you validated correctness beyond unit tests.

Typical themes: digital channels, secure authentication, transaction integrity, platform modernization, and enterprise stakeholder management.
