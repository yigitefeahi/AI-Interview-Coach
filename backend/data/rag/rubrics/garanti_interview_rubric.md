# Garanti BBVA Interview Rubric Signals

Garanti BBVA and Garanti Teknoloji-style interviews combine digital product delivery with banking-grade reliability, security, and regulatory awareness. Strong candidates show they understand that financial software errors have customer trust and compliance consequences, not only technical inconvenience.

Technical loops may cover digital banking channels, payment flows, core-banking integration, fraud controls, data platforms, or mobile backends depending on the team. Strong answers explain idempotency, auditability, access control, encryption boundaries, rollback plans, and how changes are validated before customer impact. Weak answers treat banking features like generic CRUD apps without risk analysis.

Behavioral interviews look for ownership, cross-functional work with risk/compliance/product, and calm incident handling. Strong STAR answers include fraud prevention, downtime reduction, transaction accuracy, customer complaint reduction, or successful delivery under audit constraints.

Red flags: ignoring KVKK/PCI-style sensitivity, no mention of reconciliation or audit trails, hand-waving security, or inability to explain personal contribution in regulated delivery.

Coaching tips: lead with customer trust and correctness, then architecture, then controls and monitoring. Mention reconciliation, approval flows, and staged rollout for money-moving features.

Common themes: digital onboarding, payments, fraud monitoring, mobile reliability, legacy integration, and regulated release processes.
