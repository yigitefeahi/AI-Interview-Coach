# Getir Interview Rubric Signals

Getir-style interviews prioritize speed, operational efficiency, practical engineering, and rapid learning in a hypergrowth environment. Strong candidates show they can ship under tight deadlines while maintaining acceptable reliability for customer-facing flows.

Technical topics may include routing, inventory, dispatch, mobile APIs, real-time operations, and data pipelines. Strong answers connect engineering choices to delivery time, order accuracy, or unit economics. Weak answers over-engineer without understanding operational constraints.

Behavioral answers should show ownership during incidents, cross-team coordination, and metric-driven improvement in fast-moving teams.

Coaching tips: emphasize quick iteration, monitoring, rollback, and measurable ops impact rather than theoretical perfection.
