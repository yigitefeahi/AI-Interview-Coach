# Hepsiburada Interview Rubric Signals

Hepsiburada interviews focus on marketplace scale, customer experience, seller ecosystem support, and reliable e-commerce platforms. Strong candidates explain performance, consistency, and business impact during peak commerce periods.

Technical depth may appear in catalog, checkout, payments, promotions, logistics integration, or data platforms. Strong answers include failure handling, caching strategy, and observability for customer journeys. Weak answers ignore seller-side edge cases or peak traffic planning.

Behavioral loops value collaboration between product, engineering, and operations with clear outcomes such as conversion, NPS proxy metrics, defect reduction, or fulfillment reliability.

Coaching tips: connect technical design to buyer and seller outcomes; mention campaign readiness and postmortems when discussing incidents.
