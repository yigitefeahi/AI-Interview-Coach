# Havelsan Interview Rubric Signals

Havelsan interviews emphasize mission-critical software, systems integration, security mindset, and verification discipline similar to other defense-technology employers in Türkiye. Strong candidates explain requirements traceability, robust testing, and reliable delivery in complex programs.

Technical answers should cover architecture, integration interfaces, performance under constraint, and operational support. Weak answers lack verification detail or treat security and reliability as afterthoughts.

Behavioral signals include teamwork across engineers and domain experts, accountability, and continuous improvement after incidents or audit findings.

Coaching tips: highlight documentation, review processes, test evidence, and personal ownership within program constraints.
